Thailand & Cambodia

Southeast Asia · 2023
Bangkok ·· Chiang Mai ·· Elephant Nature Park

Bangkok

Quick Takeaways
  • Hire a tuk tuk driver for the day to take you to the different temples
  • Don't shy away from using the SkyTrain — it's really easy

Chiang Mai

Quick Takeaways
  • Do not miss hiking the Monk's Trail to Wat Pha Lat
  • Sunday Night Market (Tha Pae Walking Market) — starts 4pm, largest of the week
  • Night Bazaar takes place every night

Elephant Nature Park

Elephant Nature Park is truly a magical place and a must see! Located about an hour north of Chiang Mai, it was established by Saengduean Chailert (Lek) — we even got to meet her!

We did the overnight stay and highly recommend it. The second day was the best — we hiked into the mountains, just the three of us and our guide, and saw many elephants in their natural habitat.
