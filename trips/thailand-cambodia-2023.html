Thailand & Cambodia

Southeast Asia · 2023
Bangkok ·· Chiang Mai ·· Elephant Nature Park

Bangkok

Quick Takeaways
  • Hire a tuk tuk driver for the day to take you to the different temples and wait while you explore
  • Don't shy away from using the SkyTrain — it's really easy to figure out

Chiang Mai

Quick Takeaways
  • Do not miss hiking the Monk's Trail to Wat Pha Lat
  • Sunday Night Market (Tha Pae Walking Market) — largest of the week, starts 4pm
  • Smaller market on Saturday nights; Night Bazaar takes place every night

Elephant Nature Park

Elephant Nature Park is truly a magical place and a must see! Located about an hour north of Chiang Mai, it was established by Saengduean Chailert (Lek) — we even got to meet her!

The park has rescued elephants from cruel practices. It felt like Jurassic Park as the elephants wander free!

We did the overnight stay and highly recommend it. The second day was the best — we hiked into the mountains with just our guide and saw many elephants in their natural habitat.
