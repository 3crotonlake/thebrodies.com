Toledo

Quick Takeaways
  • Stop at Mirador del Valle for a fabulous view of the city
  • The cathedral and audio guide are a must — bring your own headphones
  • You MUST eat at Botero — best meal ever! Make reservations early

Toledo Cathedral was begun in 1226 under Ferdinand III — a magnificent example of medieval Gothic architecture.

Granada

Quick Takeaways
  • Om Kalsum at C. Jardines, 17 — wonderful Moroccan tapas. Order the meatballs!
  • Only city left in Spain where you get a free tapa with each drink
  • Stayed at 'The Lover's Workshop' in the old Moorish quarter near the Alhambra

Ronda

Quick Takeaways
  • Our favorite small city in Spain
  • Las Maravillas at Carrera Espanel, 12 — yummy paella, incredible view
  • Tragatá at Calle Nueva, 4 — highly recommended, will have to go back!

Sevilla

Quick Takeaways
  • Alcazar — a must see, bring a bag lunch to eat in the gardens
  • Mercado El Postigo — art market on C. Arfe, perfect for gifts
  • La Brunilda Restaurant — make a reservation
  • Bar Alfalfa — tiny restaurant on C. Candilejo, great vibe
  • You must see a flamenco show — La Casa de Flamenco is spectacular
  • SEEBYBIKE Tours — fantastic way to see the city
