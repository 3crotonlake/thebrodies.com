Spain & Portugal

2022
Madrid ·· Toledo ·· Granada ·· Córdoba ·· Ardales ·· Ronda ·· Sevilla ·· Albufeira ·· Lisboa

Madrid

Bodega de los Secretos — a wine cellar transformed into a restaurant. Restorers found 3 clandestine passageways used to smuggle goods during the Napoleonic Wars. The duck was amazing!

Mercado San Miguel — so fun! We are not beer drinkers but LOVED cerveza limón.

Toledo

Quick Takeaways
  • Stop at Mirador del Valle for a fabulous view of the city
  • The cathedral and audio guide are a must — bring your own headphones
  • You MUST eat at Botero — best meal ever! Make reservations early

Toledo Cathedral was begun in 1226 under Ferdinand III — a magnificent example of medieval Gothic architecture. We stayed in 'The Cave' Airbnb — an incredible spot in the old city.

Granada

Quick Takeaways
  • Om Kalsum at C. Jardines, 17 — wonderful Moroccan tapas. Order the meatballs!
  • Only city left in Spain where you get a free tapa with each drink
  • Stayed at 'The Lover's Workshop' in the old Moorish quarter near the Alhambra

Córdoba

A stunning historic city — the Mezquita is breathtaking.

Ardales

A spectacular natural park in Málaga province — incredible hiking and scenery.

Ronda

Quick Takeaways
  • Our favorite small city in Spain
  • Las Maravillas at Carrera Espanel, 12 — yummy paella, incredible view from our back deck
  • Tragatá at Calle Nueva, 4 — highly recommended, will have to go back!

Sevilla

Quick Takeaways
  • Alcazar — a must see, bring a bag lunch to eat in the gardens
  • Mercado El Postigo — art market on C. Arfe, perfect for gifts
  • La Brunilda Restaurant — make a reservation
  • Bar Alfalfa — tiny restaurant on C. Candilejo, great vibe
  • You must see a flamenco show — La Casa de Flamenco is spectacular
  • SEEBYBIKE Tours — fantastic way to see the city

Algarve, Portugal

Quick Takeaways
  • Boat ride of caves is a must — make sure the boat has rubber sides. We used AllBoats
  • Tavira is a small coastal town definitely worth a visit
  • If in Albufeira, have a meal at Prazeres

Lisboa

The end point of a spectacular road trip. Lisboa deserves its own extended stay.
