London

England · 2022

London

Quick Takeaways
  • Stay in Covent Garden — great neighborhood for a first visit
  • Take a bicycle tour — Classic London Landmarks with Viator
  • Eat at Ottolenghi's NOPI — book way in advance
  • Dishoom in Covent Garden — gorgeous restaurant, book in advance
  • Sky Garden — free but needs reservations at skygarden.london
  • Portobello Market and The Distillery for Portobello Gin
  • No need for metro card — use your credit card directly on the tube
  • Fentiman Arms (64 Fentiman Road) — best pub food ever
  • Seven Dials Market (35 Earlham St) — fun multi-level food court
  • Cocktail making class at The Alchemist — so fun!
  • SUSHISAMBA — highest outdoor dining terrace in Europe
  • Tower of London: Beefeater tour free with admission, use Middle Drawbridge entrance

Our Beefeater at the Tower was the first woman to hold that position — she was fabulous!
