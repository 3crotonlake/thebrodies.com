The Olympic

Ingredients
  • 1 oz brandy
  • 1 oz Cointreau
  • 1 oz fresh orange juice
  • ¼ oz fresh lemon juice
  • Orange twist (for garnish)
Method

Combine all liquid ingredients in a shaker and give it a vigorous ten-second shake. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with an orange twist.
